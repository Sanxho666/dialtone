--- layout: page title: What is Dialtone? ---

Dialtone is a design system for Dialtone and UC.

A design system is a collection of common styles, components and associated usage documentation. These styles and components will be usable anywhere within the Dialpad and UC projects.

The aim of Dialtone is to help ensure consistency of design across all products whilst also reducing time spent building new functionality by providing reusable classes and components.
